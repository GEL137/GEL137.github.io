Tourist Spots in Mountain Province, Philippines

Explore nature, culture, and history in this beautiful province.

Bomod-Ok Falls

A stunning waterfall accessible by a scenic trek through lush Cordillera mountains. Visitors can enjoy the refreshing crystal-clear waters and verdant surroundings.

Kiltepan

Known for breathtaking sunrises and a magical sea of clouds covering the valley below, Kiltepan is a must-visit vantage point.

Marlboro Hill

A popular trekking spot offering panoramic views of rolling fields and hills, reminiscent of iconic Marlboro countryside landscapes.

Sumaguing Cave

One of the most famous caves in Sagada, offering an adventurous spelunking experience through impressive rock formations.

Hanging Coffins of Sagada

A unique cultural attraction where coffins are placed hanging on cliffs, reflecting ancient burial traditions of the Igorot people.

Maligcong Rice Terraces

Beautiful and perfectly engineered stone rice terraces showcasing traditional farming practices passed down through generations.

Bontoc Village Museum

Located in Bontoc, this museum displays Igorot artifacts and offers insight into the indigenous culture and traditions.
